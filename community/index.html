Community

Here are some active Minecraft resources. Unfortunately, we don't have room to include every community website that gets suggested to us, but if you'd like to submit yours for consideration, please email communityrequests@mojang.com with as much information as possible.

If you're having issues getting Minecraft to work, please visit our Support Center.

Official Resources

  • @jeb_ – Jeb is the lead developer of Minecraft, follow him! The rest of the Mojang team are pretty great too.
  • Facebook – Join our community on Facebook
  • Minecraft forums – A great place to talk Minecraft with fans.
  • Minecraft wiki – Learn anything and everything about Minecraft

iOS Apps

Pocket Edition is great, but what if you want even more Minecraft on your iOS device?

  • Minecraft Explorer Pro – The official reference guide for players on all platforms. Learn about items, search for servers, discuss Minecraft, and more...
  • Minecraft Seeds Pro – Find the best seeds as hand-picked by the community. Supports all versions of the game
  • Minecraft Skin Studio – Design, upload, and rate skins for the PC/Mac version of Minecraft. There's a massive library already waiting
  • Minecraft Reality – Place your creations in real world locations and view them through your iOS device, thanks to 13th Lab's wonder-tech. Truly impressive stuff
  • Minecraft Papercraft Studio – Make real world 3D models from your favorite Minecraft characters. Arts and crafts has never been this much fun!
